About Us

B&E Scoops and Slices is the go-to local spot for great food and incredible ice cream selection. Whether you're in the mood for savory favorites or a sweet treat, you'll find fresh options, fast service, and a welcoming atmosphere every day of the week.

With easy parking and a menu built for families, friends, and summer cravings, it's no surprise locals keep coming back.
